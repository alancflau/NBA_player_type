# NBA Player Type

## Motiviation:

The traditional five player positions (point guard, shooting guard, small forward, power forward, center) oversimplify the skill sets of NBA players in modern NBA. Players are hold into one of the five positions for title purposes but does not accurately define a player's specific skill set.

Coaches, scouts, players, avid viewers of NBA have already recognized players as hving skills that surpass the traditional predefinied positions and have created new terms to describe players. 

## Examples are listed below:
 - 3 And D Players (a player with who can shoot rom downtown and guard three or four positions)
 - 3-Point Specialist (a player who is desginated to shoot purely 3s)
 - Floor Generals
